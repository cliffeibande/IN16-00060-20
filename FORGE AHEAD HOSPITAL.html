BACKGROUNG INFORMATION


Established in 2000, the Forge Ahead clinics in Kenya, Tanzania and Uganda consists of acute care facilities offering quality health care. The clinics provides normal medical services, specialist clinics and high-tech diagnostic services, and has a well-equipped 24-hour Emergency Department. In 2008, the Forge Ahead clinic located in Nairobi, Kenya was awarded the ISO 9001:2000 certificate. A top priority certificate awarded only when a hospital's clinical, diagnostic, administrative and support services conform to the ISO standards.

Presently, the clinics offers services in the areas of Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynecology and Acute Care. They also provide 24-hour emergency / ambulatory services. This is further supported by basic to intermediate specialized investigations both in laboratory and radiology and a well-stocked pharmacy, all on a 24-hour basis. The Clinics has further introduced hemodialysis, dental, ophthalmology, endoscopy, histopathology and imaging services, including image-guided non-vascular intervention, CT scans and Doppler ultrasounds.
