ABOUT

Forge Ahead Health System is a family of clinics, hospitals, and other health care facilities with a physical presence in 17 communities in Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda, serving more than 300,000 patients each year. Our providers bring the knowledge and expertise of Forge Ahead Clinic to these communities and many surrounding areas to ensure our patients receive world-class health care close to home.

As a result, patients have access to a full spectrum of health care options, with more than 200 medical and surgical services and specialties available throughout the system. To best meet the unique needs of the communities it serves, patients receive quality health care at Forge Clinic Health System sites and, when needed, can receive highly specialized care at Forge Ahead Clinic's campus in Nairobi, Kenya.
